# Under the hood

Greppo is our contribution back to the geospatial community. We want to make the development of geosaptial apps easy, to make it easy for data-scientists to showcase their work. 

Greppo is built on open-source. Under-the-hood, it uses many open-source projects. Here is the list of the major ones:

* [Starlette](https://github.com/encode/starlette)
* [VueJS](https://github.com/vuejs/vue)
* [Leaflet](https://github.com/Leaflet/Leaflet)
* [Geopandas](https://github.com/geopandas/geopandas)
* [leaflet-draw](https://github.com/Leaflet/Leaflet.draw)
* [ChartJS](https://github.com/chartjs/Chart.js)
* [TailwindCSS](https://github.com/tailwindlabs/tailwindcss)
* [vue-showdown](https://github.com/meteorlxy/vue-showdown)
* [Rasterio](https://github.com/rasterio/rasterio)
* [Pillow](https://github.com/python-pillow/Pillow)
* [Shapely](https://github.com/shapely/shapely)
* [Geoapandas/xyzservices](https://github.com/geopandas/xyzservices)
